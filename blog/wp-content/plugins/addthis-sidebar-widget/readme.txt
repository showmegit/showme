AddThis feed subscription and social bookmarking buttons for your sidebar.

== Description ==

This widget adds the feed subscription and/or social bookmarking buttons from AddThis to your sidebar. This is a convenient way for visitors to subscribe to your feed or bookmark your pages without cluttering the sidebar with a gazillion buttons for all the different feed readers and social bookmark sites.

== Installation ==

1. For Wordpress 2.0/2.1, make sure that you have the [widgets plugin](http://automattic.com/code/widgets/) installed.
1. Download the plugin package
1. Extract it in the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Visit Presentation/Widgets to add the button(s) to your sidebar
